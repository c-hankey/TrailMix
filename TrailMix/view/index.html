OUR MISSION

At Pine Hiking Supplies we aim to provide quality and authentic products. Not only are they built-to-last,
they are built-to-sustain. Every time you buy a product a percentage of the profits go directly to forest
conservation. We use recycled materials for all of our products and the quality is always guaranteed or
your money back. We sell boots, jackets, hats and much more. Our products are perfect for any season.
